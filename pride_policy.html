Pride Policy

We hate it when something we bought just a year or two suddenly breaks. Your instrument ought to last many years, if not decades with normal use. We don't believe in cutting corners to save a few pennies at the expense of durability and longevity.

That's why we build instruments as though we were going to own them.

This philosophy translates into a Six-Year Guarantee that's the longest in the industry. Many of our instruments that have been in the field for over 20 years without servicing.

Our commitment to you and ourselves, is to make instruments that work all the time and every time, just as we would expect them to.
